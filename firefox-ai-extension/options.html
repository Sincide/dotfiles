🤖 AI Dynamic Colors

Real-time AI-optimized color themes from your wallpaper

🎉 Welcome to AI Dynamic Colors!

This extension brings AI-powered color optimization to your browsing experience. Colors are dynamically generated from your wallpaper and optimized for harmony and accessibility.

🚀 Quick Setup

For best results with Martin's dotfiles setup:

  1. Start the color server: cd ~/dotfiles && python3 local-color-server.py
  2. Test with your wallpaper selector: ./scripts/wallpaper-selector.sh assets/wallpapers/dark/evilpuccin.png
  3. Enable real-time monitoring: ./scripts/ai/realtime-firefox-integration.sh &

🎨 How It Works

Your existing AI pipeline now automatically updates Firefox with scientifically optimized colors that maintain harmony and accessibility standards.
